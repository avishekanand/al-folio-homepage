Avishek Anand is an Associate Professor in the [Web Information Systems (WIS)](https://www.wis.ewi.tudelft.nl/) at the [Software Technology](https://www.tudelft.nl/ewi/over-de-faculteit/afdelingen/software-technology) department at [Delft University of Technology](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/) (TU Delft). His research aims to develop *intelligent* and *transparent* machine learning approaches to help humans find relevant information.
